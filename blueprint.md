# **App Name**: CatastralPro Solutions

## Core Features:

- Homepage Navigation: Navigation structure with links to: Home, Services, Process Management, and Contact.
- Interactive Service Cards: Interactive cards for 'Boundary Modification', 'New Construction Declaration', 'Discrepancy Correction', and 'Ownership Change'.
- Document Submission Form: Form for clients to select their process type and upload documents (PDFs, JPG/PNG, and GML files).
- Google Maps Integration: Integrate a Google Maps container for clients to preview their parcel with a sample marker.
- WhatsApp Button: Floating WhatsApp button for urgent boundary-related queries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#1A237E) for a professional and serious tone.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to provide a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) for interactive elements and calls to action, providing a contrast against the dark blue.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font with a modern and neutral look for headlines and body text.
- Responsive design to ensure the website looks good on all devices, especially mobile.
- Use minimalist icons that are appropriate for a corporate and professional image.
- Subtle animations for service card interactions to provide visual feedback without being distracting.
